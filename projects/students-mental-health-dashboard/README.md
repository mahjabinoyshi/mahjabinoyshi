# Students Mental Health Dashboard – A Statistical Approach

Jun 2025 – Jun 2025  

This project is an interactive Students Mental Health Dashboard designed to monitor and analyze key indicators affecting student well‑being. The dashboard supports data‑driven decision‑making for educators, counselors, and administrators on campus.

## Project overview

- Tracks core mental health indicators such as stress levels, anxiety trends, and support resource utilization.
- Provides real‑time insights to help identify at‑risk student groups and emerging patterns in well‑being.
- Empowers educators and counselors to take timely, evidence‑based actions to improve mental health support on campus.

## Objectives

- **Monitor** student mental health using structured survey and usage data.
- **Analyze** statistical patterns in stress, anxiety, and help‑seeking behavior.
- **Inform** campus stakeholders with clear, actionable visualizations.
- **Support** data‑driven policy and resource allocation decisions.

## Features

- Interactive dashboard with filters by time period, demographic groups, and academic factors.
- Visualizations for:
  - Stress and anxiety level distributions.
  - Trends over time in well‑being indicators.
  - Utilization of counseling and mental health support services.
- Summary metrics to highlight key risks and improvement areas.
